Parks and Trails

Need to go on a nice walk and enjoy some sunlight or are you looking to for some nice grass to have a picnic on? Look no further



Sunnybrook Park

Last Updated: May 4, 2021

Location: 1132 Leslie St, Toronto, ON


Located at the corner of Leslie St and Lawerence Ave E, Sunnybrook Park is one of the most gigantic parks in all of the Greater Toronto Area with various trails and fields for you to use. It has one of the most beautiful landscapes I have ever seen with lots of forest, trees and ravines to sightsee as you walk or bike through the trails.

Is Biking or Hiking your thing? Look no further as the multiple trails at the parks can suit your needs. Or if taking your dog for a walk is what you seek, you can find an off-leash dog park to take them to roam around. Outdoor fitness is also another possibility as there is outdoor workout equipment here for everyone to use.

This park also has various sports fields for you to play soccer, football or any type of field sport you may want. Here you rarely have to worry about fields being taken up as the big acres of land should be sufficient for most people to do their own thing. Unlike other places, this one has a Cricket pitch field if that sport is what you are looking for. The options are endless here!
