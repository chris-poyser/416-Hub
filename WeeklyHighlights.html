Heart Sushi

Last Updated: May 4, 2021

Location: 1800 Sheppard Ave E Unit 2018, North York, ON M2J 5A7

Heart Sushi is one of my favourite all-you-can-eat sushi places. They have various delicious sashimi and sushi rules and their own very known special that is sushi in the shape of the heart. My favourite things to get here are the Salmon Avocado Roll, Dynamite Roll and Spicy Crispy Hand Roll. They also have a lot more than sushi that you can get on the buffet menu from soup to tempura and dumplings. They even have delicious teriyaki that they grill on the spot before they serve it to you. Desserts are located at the dessert bar where they have delicious tiramisu, pudding and lovely ice cream (mango is by far my favourite).

If the buffet is not your thing, you can also order individual items or a la carte for just specific sushi items or soup noodles if that is more of your thing. They have two pricing tiers for the buffet. Lunch is from 11 am-3:30 pm and dinner after that until close. Lunch is around $20.99-$22.99 for adults depending on the day of the week and dinner is $30.99-$33.99 also dependent on the day of the week.
